Kobe Bryant

Mamba Mentality

A Kobe Mural in Australia

Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, he played his entire 20-season professional career in the league with the Los Angeles Lakers.

Bryant won many accolades:

  • Five NBA championships
  • 18-time All-Star
  • 15-time member of the All-NBA Team
  • 12-time member of the All-Defensive Team
  • 2008 NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP)
  • Two-time NBA Finals MVP winner

2000 - 2002

With his teammate Shaquille O'Neal, he led the Lakers to three consecutive NBA championships from 2000 to 2002.

2008 & 2012

He won two gold medals as a member of the U.S. national team.

2009 - 2010

He led the team to two consecutive championships, and was named NBA Finals MVP on both occasions.
He became the all-time leading scorer in Lakers franchise history on February 1, 2010, surpassing Jerry West.

2018

He won the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for his 2017 film Dear Basketball.

2020

He died at age 41, along with his 13-year-old daughter Gianna and seven others, in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California.
